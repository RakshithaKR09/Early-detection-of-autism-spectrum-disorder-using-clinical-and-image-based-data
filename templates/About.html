Our Mission

Our mission is to help healthcare providers accurately diagnose autism spectrum disorders (ASD) at an early stage. By utilizing clinical behavioral data and image-based analysis, we aim to deliver a comprehensive and innovative solution for autism detection. Early diagnosis plays a key role in improving treatment outcomes and enhancing the quality of life for individuals with autism.

Key Features of the Autism Detection Portal

Clinical Data Analysis

We analyze clinical data including cognitive tests, behavioral assessments, and medical history to identify patterns associated with autism.

Image-Based Diagnosis

Our platform uses advanced image analysis techniques to assess visual markers of autism, offering additional layers of diagnostic information.

Early Detection

We provide early detection capabilities that allow healthcare professionals to begin treatment sooner, potentially leading to better outcomes for individuals on the autism spectrum.

How the Portal Works

Step 1: Upload Clinical Data

Users can upload a range of clinical data such as medical history, cognitive assessments, and behavior reports, which we use to analyze potential autism spectrum disorder (ASD) patterns.

Step 2: Upload Images for Analysis

In addition to clinical data, the portal supports image uploads (e.g., brain scans or facial expressions) for advanced image processing to help detect visual cues of autism.

Step 3: Generate Prediction

Once the clinical and image data are processed, the portal generates a prediction on the likelihood of autism, helping healthcare providers make informed decisions for early intervention.
